Know your berries!

Berries are there to be used

You can recover your pokemons from different status and really improve your battle skills.

Aspear Berry

Cures freeze.

Cheri Berry

Cures paralysis.

Chesto Berry

Cures sleep.

Leppa Berry

Restores 10 PP.

Pecha Berry

Cures poison.

Rawst Berry

Cures burn.
